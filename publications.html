Impact of Linear Dimensionality Reduction on Machine Learning Performance: A Case Study on Fashion-MNIST

Computing Conference 2026 · Accepted

This paper provides a systematic analysis of how PCA, LDA, Factor Analysis, and Random Forest-based feature selection affect the accuracy and efficiency of downstream classifiers on the Fashion-MNIST benchmark. We find that SVM with PCA achieves the highest accuracy (0.8995) while dramatically reducing training time. The work offers practical guidelines for selecting dimensionality reduction techniques based on classifier type and dataset characteristics.

Design of a Low-Cost, High-Resolution Underwater Acoustic Imager with Integrated Deep Learning Enhancement

IEEE Sensors Journal · Jun – Sep 2025

We present an Acoustic Synthetic Aperture Imager (ASAI) designed for shallow-water environments, enhanced with a lightweight U-Net (~1M parameters) for real-time image super-resolution. The deep learning pipeline improves axial resolution by 7–9× and lateral resolution by 2–3× over the baseline system, while maintaining a low-cost hardware footprint suitable for field deployment. The model runs in real-time on embedded hardware through quantization and pruning optimizations.
